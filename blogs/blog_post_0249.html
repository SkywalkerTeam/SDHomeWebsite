今日看房： Miro Circle

Miro is a community located right passing 15 from Mira Mesa Bldv. It closes to freeway yet not too much freeway noise.The houses built in 2001 which is fairly new.




There are townhouses and looks-like-single family houses in the community. Townhouses price ranged about 400K to 500K and about 1100 sf to 1458 sf. Single family (looks like) ones price ranged about 550k to 600k and about 1970 sf.
